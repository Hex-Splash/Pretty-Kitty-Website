WHAT IS PRETTY KITTY?

Pretty Kitty is an NFT Social Club on Solana. A Pretty Kitty NFT is your proof of membership, granting you access to special utilities and events in the discord, airdrops, community and access to future collections.

Pretty Kitty is a deflationary collection of 555 kitties, which will live, grow and progress on the blockchain. Each kitty is your unique pet and friend and all of them have individual traits and genetics.

We want to be seen as a bluechip project, which will grow and develop not as only NFT.

ROADMAP

1. Mint date, whitelist pull
We give 111 whitelist spots, make collaborations, lauch events and shill contest. You can search for whitelist requirements in our discord.
2. Sales start
Presale and public sale. When all NFTs will be minted we launch secondary sales. Our NFTs will be available on Magic Eden and Solsea. Also, we will lauch our discord rarity bot and list on SolRarity.
3. Creating and lauching token
We make token and staking. Then we will create store and add utility for the token. Tokenomics will be released too.
4. Blockchain game
We develop blockchain play-to-earn game in city building genre. It will use our NFTs and token. Some holders will be invited to the beta test.
5. Generation+
For engaging new players and giving new utilities for current NFTs we will lauch new collections. Previous collection holders are garaunteed for new collections airdrops.
6. Breeding and dressing up
Coming for new utilities. You will be allowed to breed you cats for tokens, if you have cats from different collections. Detailes will be announced at this roadmap point.
